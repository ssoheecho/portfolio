Born out of a memory from when I was younger, lucid dream recreates a dream sequence where I was surrounded by little rubber fish floating around in a museum room. Built in Unity and overlaid with text from Ezra and Miriam Elia’s book, We Go to the Gallery, the project captures the vivid surreal mesmerizing visuals.
