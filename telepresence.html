Project Management | Social Media | Content Design

Telepresence is a cyber expo hosted by the Parsons Design + Technology Class of 2021 MFA students to celebrate their work at the end of their first year in the program. Select students participated in the three-day event that culminated to a project exhibition page, student-led workshops, and series of artist talks. I helped project manage the event and was responsible for copyediting the social media content and exhibition site. You can visit the exhibition website here.
